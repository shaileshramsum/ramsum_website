Cancellation and Refund

Cancellation Policy: No refunds will be issued. Accidental or Duplicate Purchase: If you can demonstrate that your registration was a duplicate or an unintended purchase, Ramsum may, at its sole discretion, consider issuing a refund. This requires you to submit a written request with clear and verifiable proof of the error within 2 hours of your registration. Refund Policy: Should your situation qualify for an exception under the cancellation policy, you may request a refund by submitting a detailed explanation and supporting evidence (e.g., screenshots, bank statements) to contact@ramsum.in within 2 hours of your registration. Processing Time: If Ramsum approves your refund request, please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account. Right to Withhold Refunds: Ramsum reserves the right to withhold refunds in cases of suspected fraudulent activity or violation of our terms and conditions.
