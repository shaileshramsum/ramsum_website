Meet Our Team

Ashutosh Tripathi

CEO & Co-founder

"Ashutosh Tripathi, a native of Bahraich, Uttar Pradesh, received his education in Lucknow and Delhi. He worked in many small-sized Indian companies before embarking on a successful career in multinational companies such as Steria and UHG. Besides his professional journey in MNCs, he has played a pivotal role in mentoring numerous aspiring entrepreneurs, guiding them through the complexities of business launch and growth. Witnessing firsthand the struggle of small businesses to establish a robust online presence due to financial constraints, Ashutosh founded Ramsum.in. Renowned for its excellence in online marketing and video production, Ramsum.in stands out with its groundbreaking 'Rent a Website' service. This innovative offering empowers small business owners by providing them with affordable and effective solutions to enhance their online visibility and competitiveness."
